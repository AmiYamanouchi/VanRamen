Top 9 Restaurants

Introducing all the ramen you can eat in Vancouver, which is a fierce battleground for ramen. Each person has his or her own taste for ramen, but please use this as a reference to find your favorite!

SANTOUKA

A chain restaurant in Hokkaido, which has been very popular since it entered Vancouver in 2010. They purchase everything that determines the taste of ramen from Japan, and have a commitment to "faithfully reproduce the taste of Japan."

GO Website

HORIN

Horin Ramen opened in the fall of 2018. It became a hot topic that a long-established ramen shop founded in Showa 41 in Fukuoka Prefecture came to Vancouver. It is said that Feng Rin invented the first ramen with pepper in Japan, and you can enjoy pork bone ramen with pepper in Vancouver.
